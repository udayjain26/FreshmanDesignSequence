FAQ Section

What is Find A Partner?

Find a partner is a website designed to help students here at Drexel find people to hang out with. College is about the experience and creating the connections that you need to be successful as well as meeting others. We accomplish this through having users post events such as a Phillies game or to even go eat at Urban. In addition to just posting events, you’ll be able to make comments to ask about the event or to even say that you’re attending. Find a Partner will help fix your issues of not knowing anyone easily by getting you out there and exploring with your local students.

Why start Find a Partner?

As current freshman, we understand the struggle when first coming into Drexel and knowing nobody. It can be difficult to find people who you would click with and expand your friend group when you don’t know where to start. We didn’t want others to go through this same dreadful feeling of struggling to make friends in the first couple weeks.

Can anyone use this website?

Currently we have designed it for only Drexel students but in the future, we may expand this to incorporate students from other schools as well.
